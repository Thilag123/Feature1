Briyani Recipe

Let's see the Briyani recipe

Ingredients to be added

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Homemade Masala
  • Turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice: Rinse and cook basmati rice until it's 70-80% cooked. Drain the water and set it aside.
  2. Prepare the meat: Marinate chicken or mutton with yogurt, spices, and herbs for at least 30 minutes to enhance the flavor.
  3. Fry the onions: Slice onions thinly and fry them in oil until golden brown, then set aside for garnishing
  4. Layer and cook: In a large pot, layer cooked rice, marinated meat, and fried onions. Cook on low heat (dum) for about 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
